Our mission is simple: to publish writers’ best work outside their primary genre. In pushing our boundaries and moving beyond our comfort zones, we create the possibility for diverse, exciting, and unexpected results.

So, if you consider yourself a “fiction writer,” we want to see your poems or your CNF or your visual art. If you’re a “poet,” send us your short stories, graphic narratives, etc. etc.

Surprise us by surprising yourself.
