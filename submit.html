The What

genre2 accepts previously unpublished fiction, creative nonfiction, poetry, visual art, graphic narrative, and other genres that we’re not hip enough to think of at the moment. If it’s not your main gig, we want it.

 The How

To submit, please send 3–6 poems, fiction or nonfiction of 7500 words or less (flash welcome!), or up to 10 images to genre2mag@gmail.com.

The When

We publish two issues a year and read year 'round. We aim to respond to all work within two months. Also, simultaneous subs are welcome, though please send only one submission at a time.

We invite submissions from all writers interested in testing their limits.
